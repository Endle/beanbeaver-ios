## PP-OCRv5 text-detection, recognition and orientation models

The three `.onnx` files under `models/` are converted from the PP-OCRv5 mobile
models published by the PaddlePaddle / PaddleOCR project.

    Copyright (c) PaddlePaddle Authors
    Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0
    https://github.com/PaddlePaddle/PaddleOCR

The full Apache License 2.0 text appears under "Apache License 2.0" below.

through the `ort` crate.
